The effect of covid19 on societal gatherings and the absence of concerts and shows that contributed to the earnings of African entertainers led me the thought of creating a space that could fill in that gap and transcending it into something more that could add value to the lives of people

Daniel Godwin is a Nigerian Tech-Entrepreneur and has a passion for creating innovative solutions to solve societal problems.

Daniel is the Founder/Ceo of the Multinational company- Virtual music africa. His vision is to create a virtual space that woud promote the culture of African Music and entertainment.
